Welshcakes

Ingredients

  • 225g of self-rasising flour
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 100g of butter
  • 50g of caster sugar, plus extrax for sprinkling
  • 50g of currants
  • 1 egg, beaten with 3tbsp of milk

Method

  1. Mix the flour and salt together in a large bowl and rub in the butter. Add the sugar and currants, stir well.
  2. Pour the egg mixture in and mix until you have stiff dough.
  3. Roll the dough out on a lightly floured board until 5mm (1/4 inch) thickness and stamp out rounds with a pastry/biscuit cutter.
  4. Heat the griddle over a medium heat until hot and grease with a little butter. (A heavy cast iron frying pan makes a good substitute)
  5. Cook the Welshcakes for about 3-4 minutes each side, until golden brown and have risen slightly.
