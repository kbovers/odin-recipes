Stir fry

Ingredients

  • 2 blocks of egg noodles
  • 2 tsp of sunflower oil
  • 2 cooked chicken breats
  • Any vegtables you like, peppers, carrots or onions
  • Chilli sauce (however much you like)

Method

  1. Boil the noodles according to pack instructions, then drain and toss with 1 tsp oil
  2. Heat the remaining oil in a wok and add the chicken and vegtables.
  3. Stir-fry for a few mintues till the chicken is cooked. Then add the chilli sauce and noodles and mix well.
