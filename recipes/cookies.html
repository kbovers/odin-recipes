Chocolate cookies

Ingredients for 12 cookies

  • 100g of granulated sugar
  • 165g of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 115g of unsalted melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 155g of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 110g of milk or semi-sweet chocolate chunks
  • 110g of dark chocolate chunks or your preference

Method

  1. In a large bowl, whist together the sugars, salt and butter until a paste forms with no lumps.
  2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla.
  3. Sift in the flour adn baking soda, the fold the mixture with a spatula.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chunks, then chill the dough for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a baking sheet with parachment paper.
  6. Place the dough onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet, leaving at least 4 inches (10cm) of space between cookies
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the edges have started to barely brown.
  8. Cool completely before serving.
